Total Asset View Definition

Total Asset View is to provide a holistic picture of the Cisco products that our Customers purchased for their networks, which is categorized as Hardware and Software (perpetual & subscription). This is a representation of LAND stage of the overarching Lifecycle Management Strategy (Land, Adopt, Expand, and Renew).
The default view is set to All Covered products that are shipped from 1997 till today and Uncovered products that are shipped within the last 7 years. User has the flexibility to change the shipment fiscal year range for both covered and uncovered products, using Dataset Filter located under the Global Navigation. The dataset filter is only applicable to Total Asset View tab. This is NOT and opportunity view, therefore TAO rules does not apply. Product Refresh, Renew and Attach tabs are still on TAO (Total Actionable Opportunity) business rules. Please click here for more detail on TAO business rules.
